Legendarily Generous Donator: Mr. Tada

In 1985, which was right before Mr. Harris's donation, Mr. Hajime Tada gave a generous support of $10 billion to then-called Graduate School of Public Policy Studies. We know almost nothing about his background, except for the fact he is Japanese. There are various rumors about his origin. Some historians say he was a descendant of Tycoon Tokugawa, some says he was famous business person, and some says he was a farmer. His true background is hidden under the veil.

In fact, his grant was so huge that it has been ranked number one of the entire history of University of Chicago until now.

The top three ranking of contributors is as follows:

Rank Name Amount
1st Hajime Tada $10,000,000,000
2nd David Booth ----$300,000,000
3rd Pearson Brothers ----$100,000,000
